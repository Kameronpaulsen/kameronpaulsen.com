About Me

My name is Kameron Paulsen. I obtained a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Utah in 2019. I am a software engineer with experience designinging software defined tactial datalinks for the defense industry. My industry experience includes: embedded systems development, baseband design, software defined networking, operating systems, and waveform engineering.

In my personal time I am passionate about science and technology in general. I have completed multiple projects in my free time in web and applcation development. Please take the time to check out some of my projects below!
